Security & trust

Enterprise-grade security without enterprise-grade complexity.

Runs locally

Lakecode runs on your machine. Your credentials, API keys, and workspace tokens never leave your environment.

Respects your permissions

Lakecode only sees what your platform identity can see. Unity Catalog and Snowflake RBAC — no elevated permissions, no backdoors.

Safe by default

Every action is classified before execution. Read operations run automatically. Write and destructive operations require explicit approval.

Ed25519 license keys

Authentication uses cryptographically signed license keys. No shared secrets, no tokens stored on our servers.
