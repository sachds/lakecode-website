Questions

Can I use my own API keys instead?

Yes. Lakecode supports BYOK (bring your own key) for Anthropic and OpenAI. The token limits above apply to the lakecode proxy; your own keys are unlimited.

What happens when I hit the token limit?

Lakecode will let you know you've reached the limit. You can upgrade to Pro or switch to your own API keys to continue.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. You'll keep Pro access until the end of the billing period, then revert to Free.

Do you offer team pricing?

Not yet, but it's on the roadmap. Reach out if you have a team of 5+ and we'll work something out.
