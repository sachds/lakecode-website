Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the 7-day free trial?+

Everything. All 22 workflows, full Databricks and Snowflake connectivity, chat, safety classification, and the policy engine. You bring your own Anthropic API key or connect via Databricks Model Serving. No credit card required.

What's the difference between Managed and BYOK?+

Managed ($49/mo): We handle the LLM infrastructure. You get ~2,000 turns per month with no API key setup. BYOK ($29/mo): You connect your own Anthropic API key or Databricks Model Serving endpoint. You pay the LLM provider directly for token usage; Lakecode charges $29/mo for the platform.

What models are supported?+

Claude Sonnet 4, Claude Opus 4, and Claude Haiku 3.5/4.5 via the managed proxy or your own Anthropic key. Databricks Foundation Model API and any OpenAI-compatible endpoint (vLLM, LM Studio, Ollama) are also supported with BYOK.

How is Platform different from CLI?+

CLI is for individual engineers — runs on your laptop, connects to your workspace. Platform is for teams — deployed as a Databricks App with shared workflows, Engram (persistent institutional memory), governance/approval gates, audit trails, SSO/SAML, and role-scoped access.

What does the 2-week guided POC include?+

We deploy Lakecode Platform into your environment (or a dedicated workspace we provision), configure it for your team's workflows, connect your data sources, and work with your engineers to validate the value. At the end you have a working deployment.

Can I cancel anytime?+

CLI subscriptions are month-to-month with no commitments. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. Platform is annual licensing — terms are discussed during the POC.

What data does Lakecode access?+

Lakecode connects to your Databricks or Snowflake workspace using your credentials. It accesses job configs, run logs, query history, billing tables, and Unity Catalog metadata. With the managed proxy, only token counts are tracked for billing. With BYOK, your data goes directly to your LLM provider.
