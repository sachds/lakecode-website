How credits work

Usage-based

Each command costs based on the actual AI tokens used. Simple commands cost less, complex ones cost more.

Free daily allowance

Everyone gets $1.00/day in free credits. If you have purchased credits, free credits are used first.

No surprises

Credits are pre-purchased. You'll never get an unexpected bill. Top up when you need more.

Questions

Can I use my own API keys instead?

Yes. Lakecode supports BYOK (bring your own key) for Anthropic and OpenAI. Your own keys bypass the credit system entirely.

What happens when I run out of credits?

Free daily credits reset at midnight UTC. If you've used your daily free credits and your purchased balance is zero, you'll be prompted to buy more credits.

Do credits expire?

No. Purchased credits never expire. Use them whenever you need.

How much does a typical command cost?

Simple commands like /status cost a few cents. Complex workflows like /debug or /audit can cost $0.30–$0.80 depending on the data involved.

Do you offer team pricing?

Not yet, but it's on the roadmap. Reach out if you have a team of 5+ and we'll work something out.
