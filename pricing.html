How auto-reload works

1. Add a payment method

Securely save a card via Stripe. Your first reload amount is charged immediately.

2. Use lakecode normally

Each command deducts from your balance based on actual AI tokens used. Free credits are used first.

3. Auto top-up

When your balance drops below 20% of your reload amount, we automatically charge your card and add credits.

Questions

Can I use my own API keys instead?

Yes. Lakecode supports BYOK (bring your own key) for Anthropic and OpenAI. Your own keys bypass the credit system entirely.

What happens when I run out of credits?

If auto-reload is enabled, your card is charged and credits are added automatically. If disabled, free daily credits reset at midnight UTC. With no balance remaining, you'll be prompted to add a payment method.

Can I change or pause auto-reload?

Yes. On your dashboard you can change the reload amount, toggle auto-reload on or off, or manually trigger a reload anytime.

How much does a typical command cost?

Simple commands like /status cost a few cents. Complex workflows like /debug or /audit can cost $0.30–$0.80 depending on the data involved.

Do you offer team pricing?

Not yet, but it's on the roadmap. Reach out if you have a team of 5+ and we'll work something out.
