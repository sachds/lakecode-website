Frequently Asked Questions

How does pay-as-you-go billing work?

Pro users who use the managed proxy pay based on actual LLM usage. You add a payment method via Stripe, load credits ($20 minimum), and usage is deducted per request. Auto-reload tops up your balance when it gets low. If you bring your own API key, you pay Anthropic directly and lakecode costs nothing.

Can I use my own API key on Pro?

Yes. Pro unlocks all workflows and features regardless of how you connect to the LLM. Bring your own Anthropic key, or use our managed proxy — your choice.

What models are supported?

Claude Sonnet 4, Claude Opus 4, and Claude Haiku 3.5/4.5 via the managed proxy or your own Anthropic key. Databricks FMAPI models and any OpenAI-compatible endpoint (vLLM, LM Studio, Ollama) are also supported.

What's the difference between CLI Pro and the Databricks App?

CLI Pro is for individual developers — runs on your laptop, connects to your Databricks or Snowflake workspace. The Databricks App deployment is for teams — runs inside your workspace with SSO, approval gates, audit trail, and Unity Catalog state management.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. Your credit balance stays available until used. You can manage billing through the Stripe portal from your dashboard.

What data does lakecode access?

Lakecode connects to your Databricks or Snowflake workspace using your credentials. Workflow data (job configs, error logs, billing tables) is processed by the LLM for analysis. No data is stored on our servers.
