How it works

1. Sign up free

Create an account with Google. You'll get a license key and limited daily usage to try everything out.

2. Upgrade when ready

Save a payment method via Stripe from your dashboard. You're immediately upgraded and auto-reload is enabled.

3. Never run out

When your balance gets low, your card is automatically charged and your balance is topped up. Pause or cancel anytime.

Questions

Can I use my own API keys instead?

Yes. Lakecode supports BYOK (bring your own key) for Anthropic and OpenAI. Your own keys bypass the billing system entirely.

What happens when I hit the free daily limit?

You'll see a message that your daily usage is exhausted. It resets every 24 hours, or you can upgrade to Pro for unlimited usage.

Can I change or cancel my plan?

Yes. On your dashboard, click "Manage Billing" to update your payment method, change plans, or cancel. Changes take effect immediately.

What does auto-reload mean?

When your balance drops below 20% of your reload amount, we automatically charge your saved payment method and top up your account. Default reload is $20 (adjustable from $5 to $100). This way you never run out mid-session.

How does Team / Enterprise work?

Contact us at team@lakecode.ai and we'll set up custom pricing, dedicated onboarding, SSO, and team management tailored to your organization.
